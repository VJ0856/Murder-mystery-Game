## 🔄 Game Flow

```
1. Launch: streamlit run app.py
   ↓
2. Click: "START NEW INVESTIGATION"
   ↓
3. Wait: AI generates unique case (2-3 sec)
   ↓
4. Explore: Crime scene, suspects, clues
   ↓
5. Interrogate: Click suspect buttons (max 5)
   ↓
6. Find: Contradictions = +10 points each
   ↓
7. Accusation: Choose the murderer
   ↓
8. Results: Win or lose, see final score
   ↓
9. Repeat: Click "NEW INVESTIGATION"
```
